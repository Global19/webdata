Use well-known vocabularies/schemas so that understanding resources is well-defined.

Why?

Documents should use well-known or at least well-documented vocabularies/schemas, so that after parsing, understanding the documents is possible and well-defined. At the very least, the vocabulary/schema must make it possible to discover links in documents, so that consumers can find and follow them.
